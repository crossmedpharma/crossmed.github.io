    About Us

    A fast growing Indian Pharmaceutical Company with a vision to be the leader in Pharma Industry, we commit ourselves to total customer care by delivering world class products and services. At Crossmed, nurturing talent, providing ample opportunities for growth and ensuring all round development is the essence of our people management philosophy.

    Crossmed Pharmaceuticals is a trusted source for quality oriented drugs and quality is the hallmark of Crossmed Pharmaceuticals established in the year 2010, as a joint venture company. The company is being managed by the professionals from Multinational Pharmaceutical Companies with a very successful track record of achievements.

    Undoubtly we believe in providing the best to the Medical Fraternity and we are confident of achieving the peak of Pharmaceutical industries. All the molecules of Crossmed Pharmaceuticals are time tested and conventional.
